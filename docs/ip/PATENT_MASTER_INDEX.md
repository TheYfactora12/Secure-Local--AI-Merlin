## Alice Doctrine Warning

Since *Alice Corp. v. CLS Bank* (2014), software patents that are "abstract ideas" are invalid. Your attorney must draft claims tied to specific technical mechanisms. Your invention disclosures are already written with this in mind — emphasizing the specific pipeline components, not just the outcome.
