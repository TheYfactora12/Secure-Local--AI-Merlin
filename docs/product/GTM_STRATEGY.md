## The Pricing Model — "Get Them Hooked, Keep Them Coming"

This is the Apple model applied to AI infrastructure. Free base, paid growth, seasonal updates.

### Layer 1 — Free Base (The Hook)
- Free install for qualifying organizations (healthcare, legal, financial, education)
- You install it. They own it. No subscription required to run it.
- **The hook:** Once it's running, it's learning their data. Switching cost grows every day.

### Layer 2 — Support Package (The Relationship)

| Package | Price | What They Get |
|---|---|---|
| **Self-Managed** | $0 | Software only, community docs, no SLA |
| **Standard Support** | $99–$299/month | Updates, troubleshooting, new model drops, email SLA |
| **Managed Growth** | $499–$999/month | Full management — updates, new workflows, quarterly reviews |

### Layer 3 — Seasonal Model Updates (The Revenue Engine)
- Every quarter, new model packs drop: better reasoning, new capabilities, domain-specific fine-tunes
- Standard Support gets them automatically
- Self-Managed pays per pack: $49–$149 each
- The hook deepens: their Merlin has learned their data. New models make it smarter. They want the upgrade.
- This is the Apple annual moment — but quarterly.

### Layer 4 — Enterprise Data Sovereignty Package
- For organizations that want to own their training data and fine-tune on it
- Custom pricing: $10,000–$50,000/year
- This is the product that disrupts the AI cloud giants
